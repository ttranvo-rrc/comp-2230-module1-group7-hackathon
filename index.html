Deep Dish Pizza

Deep dish pizza is the best pizza in the world,

not because its from Chicago but because its deep. Deep meaning, deep toppings, deep cheese, deep sauce, deep soul.

If you've ever wanted to experience a pizza rather than taste it, deep dish pizza is your go-to.

Deep-dish pizza is a thick-crust pizza, synonymous with Chicago,

that is baked in a round, deep pan, creating a pie-like structure with high, buttery crust edges.

Characterized by layers of mozzarella cheese, toppings (like sausage),

and chunky tomato sauce, it features an "upside-down" construction with the cheese and toppings

at the bottom and the sauce on top. Often cooked in a cast-iron skillet,

it's a hearty dish that typically requires a knife and fork to eat due to its substantial, soft, and oozy filling.
