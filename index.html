Neapolitan Pizza

Neapolitan Pizza is a traditional Italian flatbread, It typically has minimal toppings.
It has a very thin base and a thick crust that is light and airy.
A very popular Neapolitan Pizza is the Margherita

Deep Dish Pizza

Deep dish pizza is the best pizza in the world,

not because its from Chicago but because its deep. Deep meaning, deep toppings, deep cheese, deep sauce, deep soul.

If you've ever wanted to experience a pizza rather than taste it, deep dish pizza is your go-to.

Deep-dish pizza is a thick-crust pizza, synonymous with Chicago,

that is baked in a round, deep pan, creating a pie-like structure with high, buttery crust edges.

Characterized by layers of mozzarella cheese, toppings (like sausage),

and chunky tomato sauce, it features an "upside-down" construction with the cheese and toppings

at the bottom and the sauce on top. Often cooked in a cast-iron skillet,

it's a hearty dish that typically requires a knife and fork to eat due to its substantial, soft, and oozy filling.

Hawaiian Pizza

As originated in Canada, the pineapple topped pizza has people on the divide for if it belongs or not.

To be more specific, a Hawaiian pizza consists of the following:

  • Pineapples
  • Cooked ham or bacon
  • Cheese
  • Tomato sauce

It may be considered a bit controversial, which causes debates from people around the world.

Another thing about this so-called "Hawaiian" pizza is that pineapples are not native to Hawaii! However, the fruit became deeply tied with Hawaii's economy, identity, and culture, which solidified its place in Hawaii.

Even though this pizza is iffy on if it is good or bad, you never know until you try it.
